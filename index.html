Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Chania historic old town, Marvo Coffee And More is where tradition meets contemporary coffee culture. We are passionate about serving the finest specialty coffee, handcrafted with care and precision.

From our signature Freddo Espresso to traditional Greek coffee, every cup tells a story of Cretan hospitality and quality. Join us for a moment of tranquility in our welcoming space.
